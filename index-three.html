Intern-hub

Intern-hub is a platform for identifying passionate students to work with mentors or industries and execute internships with certificate. Do you wish to embark on a journey of learning, growth, and hands-on experience? Look no further! We're thrilled to introduce "Intern Hub," our exclusive internship program tailored for aspiring students from Amal Jyothi College of Engineering (AJCE).

Duration

15th June 2024 to 15th July 2024 (during the semester vacation - 2024).

Who can apply

Students or Alumni from Amal Jyothi College of Engineering, as a team or individuals.

Certification

Partnered industry and/or Startups valley TBI.

Areas of work

The passionate students can work with a mentor for research related activities like literature survey, simulation work, experiment, field survey, and book editing etc. Create a research paper (conference or journal) based on the work and publish. Programme will be certified by Startups Valley TBI.

This platform is directly linked with the industry in the specified domains. The candidates will work with a mentor, identify one technical problem from the partner industries through industry visits, develop a solution for the selected problems (Ideation). Financial support for prototyping can get through the industries or Govt organizations (Prototyping - funding). A proposal for hackathon, project contest, or idea stage funding will be the expected output along with prototype. Certificate will be issued by Startups Valley and/or industry.
